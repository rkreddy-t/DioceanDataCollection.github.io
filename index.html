About the application

The purpose of the Diocesan Data Collection Application (DDCA) is to provide a simpler process of data collection and analytics for Diocese. The Application features a single dashboard in addition to data processing and presentation. The goal is to save time in the generation of reports as needed by diocese.
Our client Diocese of Bridgeport collects data on the operations of the different parishes it oversees which encompasses financial data, parish attendances, events and activities at the different parishes and historical data.
This application was being developed on Appian to sort out our client “Diocese of Bridgeport” needs, also having an application that matches their requirements.
